About Us

We are Washington University in St. Louis' running club! We are co-ed and welcome runners of all commitments and abilities. In the fall, we compete in local and NIRCA cross country races. In the winter and spring, we compete at track meets and road races. We practice Sunday-Friday at 4:30 PM; we meet in the South Forty House lobby. In addition to practice, we also hold plenty of fun social events!

Races

  • Forest Park Cross Country Festival
  • MC5
  • ISU
